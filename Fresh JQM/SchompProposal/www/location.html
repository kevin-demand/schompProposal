WE’RE READY TO STAKE OUR CLAIM ON I-35.

The future home of BMW of South Austin.

We explored multiple locations in Southwest Austin including sites in Bee Cave, Lakeway, The Hills, Circle C Ranch and in South Austin. We believe we’ve found the perfect location: A 13.88 acre site along the I-15 corridor in South Austin. (we are currently negotiating for the purchase of the property). It leads directly to the heart of Austin and the University of Texas campus. The I-15 corridor is also home to countless hotel, retail and restaurant establishments, not to mention South Austin’s largest shopping district, Southpark Meadows. As THE primary north-to-south artery into and out of Austin, this location is also home to Volkswagen of Onion Creek, Audi of South Austin, Sterling Acura, Auto Nation Toyota of South Austin, South Point Nissan, and (soon to be built) Ferrari-Maserati of Austin.

This location is also best positioned to serve the communities of Southwest Austin and the rapidly growing cities between Austin & San Antonio.

WE PLAN TO BUILD A LEEDER

How we’d approach building a green, LEED Certified facility.

What is LEED? LEED is a third party certification program and the nationally accepted benchmark for the design, construction and operation of high performance green buildings. Developed by the U.S. Green Building Council in 2000 through a consensus based process, LEED serves as a tool for buildings of all types and sizes. LEED certification offers third party validation of a project’s green features and verifies that the building is operating exactly the way it was designed to.

13 variables that BMW of South Austin will balance to achieve LEED certification.

Our facility will meet the criteria for the 2000 unit planning volume and meet all BMW Future Retail and Corporate Identity requirements.

This location is already zoned for automotive use so the first item that must be addressed is finalizing the purchase agreement and acquiring the land. The current land owner has been approached, is a willing seller and we have started negotiations.
