One PRICE

  • Transparency — Save our customers time while providing complete transparency through the research and purchase process.
  • Fair Pricing — Through daily market research, we establish the true value of our vehicles based on three factors; age of inventory, market supply and customer demand, and then price our cars at the low-end of the pricing spectrum to ensure that we are offering a truly competitive price.
  • No D & H — Schomp Automotive Group never charges dealer handling fees or any other additional ‘processing’ or ‘documentation’ fees. Customers hate them and so do we. Our goal has always been to provide the highest level of transparency, have great prices, great service, and to sell as many cars as possible.

ONE PERSON

  • Instead of dealing with multiple people, at the Schomp Automotive Group dealerships, customers only work with ONE Sales Advisor.
  • No F & I Box — We do not believe in a separate finance offices or ‘closers’
  • A to Z sales model — From the time they walk in the door, place a phone call, or submit an internet lead, the customer deals with just ONE PERSON until the moment they leave in a new car.
  • Better People — This requires a higher caliber Sales Advisor, a commitment to continuous process evaluation and improvement, and constant training. Our Sales Advisors are compensated based on volume and customer satisfaction. They are driven to find the best car, not the car that makes them the most commission. It might not be the easiest way but it’s the Schomp way and our customers love it!

ONE HOUR

  • Once a customer has decided on their next vehicle purchase and determined the terms of the transaction, what they really want is to get behind the wheel! We value our customer’s time so our goal is to have every customer on their way within ONE HOUR.
  • No F & I Box — We do not believe in a separate finance offices or ‘closers’
  • Time Tracking —We obsess about time and have gone so far as placing timers on the deal jackets so we can track & improve the customer experience.
  • Technology — We constantly work with our vendors to improve the way technology is integrated into our sales process. Answers need to be less than three clicks away and systems must communicate with each other – by saving our Sales Advisors time we will save our customers time.
